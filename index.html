Analysis Methodology

How we transformed raw open data into actionable insights.

01

Data Ingestion

Extracted 35 years of global flood records from the RIMES/DFO Archive and infrastructure data from OpenStreetMap.

02

Spatial Analysis

Used Python and the Haversine formula to calculate distances between 2,145 health sites and 24 historic flood events in Ghana.

03

Risk Categorization

Identified facilities within a 1km "Risk Buffer" to prioritize emergency preparedness and infrastructure resilience.
